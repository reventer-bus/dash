# Franchise Partner Guide — Maker AI

## Dashboard Modules

### 1. Command Centre (Home)
Your main operational view on login:
- Active orders count
- Live printing jobs
- Post-processing queue
- Quality check queue
- Packing & dispatch queue
- Revenue today
- Active alerts

### 2. Printer Farm
Each printer card shows:
- Printer name and ID
- Current job and file name
- Print progress (%)
- Time remaining
- Material type (PLA / PETG / ABS)
- AI health score (0–100)
- Live camera feed

**Controls:** Pause | Resume | Stop | Filament Change | Report Issue

### 3. Live Camera Monitoring
The AI continuously monitors your cameras for:
- Spaghetti failures
- Layer shifts
- Warping
- Print abnormalities

You receive an alert and can: **Check → Ignore → Mark Fixed**

### 4. Order Pipeline
Orders flow through:

`NEW → AI PREP → PRINTING → POST PROCESS → QUALITY CHECK → PACK → DISPATCH`

Click any order to see customer details, 3D file, material, assigned printer, and deadline.

### 5. AI Print Assistant
Ask the AI questions:
- "Why did this print fail?"
- "What should I do next?"
- "How do I fix warping on PETG?"

The AI uses your full print history to give specific answers.

### 6. Post Processing Checklist
After printing, complete:
- [ ] Support removal
- [ ] Cleaning
- [ ] Sanding
- [ ] Assembly (if required)
- [ ] Final inspection
- [ ] Upload final image for AI verification

### 7. Packaging Assistant
Enter object dimensions → AI recommends:
- Box size
- Packing material (bubble wrap, foam, etc.)
- Dispatch checklist

### 8. Inventory
Track your stock:
- PLA / PETG / ABS filament remaining (grams per spool)
- Nozzles in stock
- Build plates
- Other consumables

Set low-stock alerts to get notified before you run out.

### 9. Maintenance
The system tracks:
- Total print hours per printer
- Belt condition score
- Nozzle wear estimate
- Lubrication schedule
- Maintenance alerts

Follow the maintenance schedule to keep your AI health score high.

### 10. Performance
Your stats:
- Jobs completed (day / week / month)
- Success rate
- Failure rate
- Average print time
- Material used

## Quality Standards

To maintain your franchise licence, maintain:
- Success rate ≥ 90%
- AI health score ≥ 80
- Response time to alerts ≤ 15 minutes
